# Grasp verification: founder statement vs compiled corpus (2026-07-08)

Source statement (Jarred, verbatim, 2026-07-08): "we would raise funding based on achievement and faith from investors we know already. we know that anthropic etc. can do this, but companies need trusted companions to bring them through this and help them get a headstart. Dominos fall as: C-levels first, chief of staff/organizer. Engineers next, revamp/overhaul. All else follows based on demand. I am the founder of this. We want to develop this in a manner in which contribution can earn equity for originating members (being them) but the vision is mine."

This statement was formalized same day as `memory-bank/decisions/0003-founder-structure-funding-positioning.md`, which self-commissioned this check. Seven sonnet lenses (funding, positioning, gtm-ladder, founder-equity, product-fidelity, corrections-propagation, voice-vision) audited the full repo against it. This document is the synthesis of those seven runs, checked back against the primary files before being written down. Every quote below was independently re-verified against the source file it cites as part of writing this synthesis.

## Overall verdict

Partial grasp, and the honest read is that this is a propagation problem, not a comprehension problem. All seven lenses came back "partial." None came back "aligned." None came back "failed." That consistency is itself the finding.

Where the corpus states the founder's intent directly, it states it well. CLAUDE.md's seven numbered corrections, `memory-bank/decisions/0003`, and the new `07-roadmap/ROADMAP.md` all restate founder structure, achievement-and-faith funding, trusted-companion positioning, and the domino GTM ladder with high fidelity, in several places nearly verbatim. No lens found the corpus inventing a position the founder never stated, and no lens found the corpus's surviving operational conclusions (scope the ask to a pilot, not a $10-50M raise; Jarred alone signs as founder; the ladder runs C-levels then engineers then org-wide demand) contradicted anywhere.

What is missing is backfill. The correction has not been walked back through the exact files this repo's own workflow treats as authoritative and as the literal raw material for the next deliverable. `01-overview/product-concept-overview-2026-07-08.md` section 12, which CLAUDE.md itself says "supersedes conflicting lines above it," and `05-counsel/counsel-verdicts-2026-07-08.md`, the panel-verdict layer decision 0003 names by category as overridden, both still contain phrases decision 0003 quotes verbatim as invalid: the check-writer and signals-inexperience funding framing, and the 55-65/20-25/10-15 fixed founder split. One counsel panelist's dissent (codex, proposing to swap "engineers" for a generic, case-study-gated "department workflow") sits un-rebutted in the same file. Three memory-bank orientation files (`activeContext.md`, `projectbrief.md`, `INTENT.md`) still say "the four authoritative corrections" when CLAUDE.md has carried seven since decision 0003 landed, so a reader who trusts the orientation files' own count has no signal to go looking for the three newest rules. CLAUDE.md's own pre-Upexi-correction caveat (cold-start ritual item 4) names only the Upexi correction for the two headline synthesis reports, not the founder-structure correction that decision 0003 says applies to the same two reports on a separate point, and it never extends to the roughly two dozen raw dossiers underneath those reports where the stale framing is most concrete (`03-execution/venture-structure.md` is literally titled "The 3-Way Founding Partnership").

The immutable evidence layer (`00-call-notes/`, `02-sweep/`, `03-execution/`, `04-competitive/`) legitimately still carries the pre-correction reasoning, as repo convention intends: corrections happen in the overview, counsel, and memory-bank layers, never by editing evidence files. That is fine in principle. The problem is that the caveat mechanism meant to warn a reader off that stale reasoning currently covers one of the two corrections it needs to cover, and names two files where it should name closer to twenty.

Because `INTENT.md`'s own journey 1 ("Build the 7/15 concept doc") reads the overview and the counsel verdicts and then extracts, this is not a someday cleanup item. It is a live dependency for the deliverable due 7/15, and the patch plan below should be treated as a prerequisite for that work, not a parallel-track nice-to-have.

## What the corpus gets right (consolidated)

**The directive layer itself**
- CLAUDE.md's seven numbered corrections restate decision 0003 (and the earlier decision 0001) with high, in places verbatim, fidelity: founder structure, achievement-plus-faith funding, trusted-companion positioning, product unnamed, Recurro as input not name, Upexi separateness, Mima exclusion.
- `memory-bank/decisions/0001` through `0003` form a clean, accretive, dated decision trail. Each is explicit about what it overrides and none rewrites a prior entry in place, which is the right audit-trail hygiene for exactly this kind of correction.
- `07-roadmap/ROADMAP.md`, the newest file in the repo, opens with a one-paragraph restatement of decision 0003 that every lens independently flagged as the most faithful rendering in the corpus, preserving even the founder's own "organizer" and "revamp/overhaul" wording that other files compress away.
- Decision 0003 anticipated its own propagation risk and self-commissioned this verification pass. That is why this document exists, and it is a good instinct to keep for future corrections.

**Structural and substantive fidelity**
- The seven founding pillars, the must-haves (reward-not-replace gamification, anti-abuse from day one, the human-gate/graduated-autonomy product law), and the domino GTM sequence are rendered consistently across the overview, the sweep dossiers, the execution and competitive dossiers, and the roadmap. No dossier invents an eighth pillar or reorders the ladder.
- The bottom-line conclusions the corrections exist to protect (a scoped pilot ask rather than $10-50M; Jarred alone as founder; the ladder running C-levels, then engineers, then demand) are not contradicted anywhere in the corpus. Every misalignment found below is about stated reasoning or a residual label in a specific sentence, never about the corpus landing on a different answer.
- Honest-math discipline (evidence tags, the 16x claim gated behind Carter's methodology memo, the output-parity fallback) is applied consistently with no overclaim found anywhere.
- Blockchain and tokenization, and the marketplace/attribution pillars, are held correctly to a horizon-only, roadmap-only status; no dossier inflates them to strengthen the near-term pitch.

**Places the corpus already self-corrected, worth reusing as the pattern**
- Overview section 2, pillar 5 models the right way to handle a stale quote: it states the original call framing ("nothing stops Salesforce/ClickUp from copying") immediately beside the counsel-verified reframe, in the same sentence, instead of leaving a reader to find the correction three sections later. This is the pattern the rest of the corpus should be edited toward, not a from-scratch invention.
- `02-sweep/swarm-meeting-capture.md` explicitly flags, on its own, that "working name candidate" framing for Recurro is superseded. `03-execution/sequencing-roadmap.md` separately anticipated the relationship-versus-institutional-diligence distinction that decision 0003 later made binding ("this is a relationship-and-vision bet, not a fund deploying LP capital under return pressure"). Both predate this swarm's run and show the correction is learnable from context, not only from a checklist, when a dossier author checks.
- Feature scorecard item 27 (sovereign/open-source stack option) already discusses Anthropic dependency in the calm, engineering-hygiene register decision 0003 asks for, even though the grey-areas section a few pages earlier states the identical underlying fact in existential terms. The right register already exists in the document; it is just not yet the only register.
- `04-competitive/competitive-risk-2026-07-08.md`'s own risk register frames single-vendor Anthropic concentration as ordinary diversification hygiene ("build a thin model-abstraction/router layer") in one row, even while a different row in the same file frames a related fact ("already materializing... more damaging form than IP theft") in maximally existential terms. Same file, same evidence base, proof the calmer register is achievable without new research.
