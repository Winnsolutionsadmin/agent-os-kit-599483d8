## Summary
Catch-up/intro call between Jarred and Nick Metzler. Demoed his personal stack (status bar, OAuth rotation cycle, dream cycle, Guardian/Doctor/Sage agents, priority queuing/focus mode, meeting ingestion wizard). Nick endorsed the output/token ratio idea but flagged the need for leagues, minimum token thresholds, anti-abuse caps, and capturing fake outliers. Strong mutual rapport; agreed to continue on gamification/AI-adoption collaboration.
