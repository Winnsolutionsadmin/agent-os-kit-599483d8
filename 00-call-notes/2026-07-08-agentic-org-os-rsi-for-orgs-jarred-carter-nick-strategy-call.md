# Agentic Org OS / RSI for Orgs — Jarred, Carter, Nick strategy call

**Date:** 2026-07-08T18:21:02Z | **Attendees:** [[Jarred Winn]], [[Carter Razink]], [[Nick Metzler]]
**Source:** recall | **Category:** product-strategy / fundraising-prep

## Summary
Three-way ideation call between Jarred, Carter Razink, and Nick Metzler on building an Agent Operating System / recursively-self-improving productivity platform for businesses. Core thesis: compress time-from-idea-to-execution via a unified, simple UI (Tinder-like swipe cards, Slack-like text interface) that manages employees via AI, ties top-level org OKRs to weighted subtasks, and rewards adoption. Discussed good/better/best packaging (C-level chief of staff → engineering dept → whole-org brain), a skills marketplace (possibly tokenized), sovereignty/open-source angle vs Anthropic capturing customer IP, OAuth rotation economics, and Carter's case study (team of 4 producing 16x baseline vs control of 18 at 2.5x). Jarred plans to pitch Alan (Upexi) for $10-50M seed. Consensus: sell C-levels first, engineering second (they drive demand across the org), consulting + software black-box hybrid model with equity + cash from clients.

## Decisions
- Jarred will draft an early deck and concept notes; shared doc for async contribution
- Pitch Alan (Upexi) end of this week or next week for potential $10M-$50M raise
- Product will be sold as good/better/best: C-level chief of staff → engineering dept → whole-org agentic OS
- Take cash + equity from client engagements, not cash alone (first-mover monopolizes vertical)
- Lead with C-level sale, then engineering team, then cascade to rest of org (engineers drive demand)
- Working name candidate: 'Recurro' (Carter's AI-suggested name for recursive self-improvement for orgs)
- Async collaboration via Telegram going forward; reconvene after Jarred meets Alan
