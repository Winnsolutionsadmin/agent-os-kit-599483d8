The vision, distilled

The canonical articulation of the thesis, distilled from the operator recordings that seeded this project. Statements below are near verbatim; each one is a design commitment, not a slogan.

Core thesis

Communication gaps between employees should be solved, and people should be spending their time being most creative.

Meetings are for creativity. Humans bounce ideas; agents grab the output and triage it. Everything else is wasted attention.

The end-state interface: one window of consequence

A single zone-screen showing what you are working on, OKR style, objective plus key results, with the agent layer putting decisions in front of you. Not a notification feed. Not seventeen terminal windows.

Triage as the new operating layer

An agent essentially manages the triage layer. The small choices, the ones where you would pick the recommended option about seven times out of ten, get absorbed by the triage agent. The human only sees the high-leverage forks. This is the flow and focus mechanism.

Visual and ambient

A persistent tally and score bar keeps the state of the operation visible at the edge of attention. The working prototype of that bar already runs daily in the reference implementation.

Agreements carried forward

  • One surface of consequence, never a feed. Decisions come to the human; the human does not hunt for them.
  • The triage layer is the product's spine. Autonomy is earned per class of decision, never assumed.
  • Adoption is designed, not mandated. Mechanics reward use; nothing threatens replacement.
