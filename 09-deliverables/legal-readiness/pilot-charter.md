## Scope (90 days, three phases)

- **Days 1 to 30: boundaries, baselines, read-only capture, and one gated workflow.** Set the boundaries and record the baselines. Capture is read-only. One gated workflow goes live behind the human gate (Approve / Edit / Skip). No write access to counterparty systems in this phase.
- **Days 31 to 60: measured recommendations and instrumented gates.** The system produces recommendations, measured against the baseline. Every action stays behind an instrumented human gate; nothing that touches money, an outside party, or anything irreversible moves without a person deciding.
- **Days 61 to 90: opt-in adoption cohort.** A voluntary cohort adopts the gamified adoption layer. Adoption is measured; participation is opt-in.

**Named pilot deliverables:** the signed baseline; the one production-grade, human-gated workflow the counterparty keeps; the day-90 evidence pack; and the 16x methodology memo (the throughput benchmark's task universe, baseline, comparison design, quality measure, and confidence, defined and held to the honest-math audit). The 16x figure is an internal benchmark scoped to its measured activity until that memo lands; it is never a customer promise.
