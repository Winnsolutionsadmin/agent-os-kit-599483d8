02 The problem

The problem is adoption, not capability.

Capability is arriving. Adoption is not. The blocker is human.

  • 94% already use AI at work. Disclose it, though, and a controlled experiment rated you 10x lazier and 24 percentage points less likely to be picked for high-visibility work, at identical output. Survey Atlassian, 2026. Employees hide the exact behavior an adoption product needs them to show.
  • The cost of waiting. Every quarter, shadow AI spreads ungoverned and the head start compounds to whoever crosses first.

The company that makes its people safe to use AI takes the vertical. Nobody's selling that yet.

09 The boundary of the pilot

During the pilot, the product turns one organization's meetings, objectives, and workflow state into sourced recommendations an executive can approve, edit, or skip.

  • Out of scope: undisclosed capture, ungated actions, anything you didn't sanction. Capture is always disclosed.
